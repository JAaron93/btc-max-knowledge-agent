## Key Concepts Summary

### Bitcoin
Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system that allows online payments without financial institutions. It uses blockchain technology for security and decentralization.

### Lightning Network
A Layer-2 payment protocol on Bitcoin enabling fast, low-cost transactions through payment channels.

### dApps
Decentralized applications running on blockchain networks, offering services without central control.

### GENIUS Act
Legislation promoting blockchain innovation and cryptocurrency adoption in the United States.
